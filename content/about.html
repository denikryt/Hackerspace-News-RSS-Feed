This site aggregates publications from hackerspace RSS feeds collected from the wiki source page.

This project was created by a guy from Ukraine who shares hackerspace values and wanted to make navigation through hackerspace news a little easier.

The information space around hackerspaces is often fragmented. Each hackerspace decides for itself where and how to publish updates, news, blog posts, event announcements, and other materials. Because of that, following what is happening across different spaces is harder than it should be.

This project tries to solve a small but practical part of that problem through RSS. If a hackerspace publishes an RSS feed and is listed on the wiki source page, its posts can appear here.

The goal is to provide a simpler way to browse hackerspace publications in one place and make it easier to navigate by hackerspace, by country, and through the global feed.

Made with passion by Nachitima.
